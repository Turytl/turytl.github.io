Ghostpoet - Oh Lord!

View Project

London, UK

Ghostpoet - Oh Lord!

2024

Music

03:40

Swipe

Taken from the twice Mercury Prize-nominated artist's new EP Am I The Change I Wish To See?, Oh Lord! was inspired by a moment of contemplation about what it would look like to see Black people enjoying themselves in Heaven. And for its part, the video takes the viewer on a communal journey that transcends the conventional depiction of Heaven.

Shortform

Set within an old car garage in London’s East End, we shot and cut a film for Icon Magazine’s cover, featuring arguably the greatest rock & roll frontman the UK has ever produced - Liam Gallagher. Stripped-back and full throttle, the film channels Gallagher’s signature swagger against the grit and grease of the garage. It’s raw, loud, and unmistakably Liam - no frills, all attitude.

London, UK

Aaron Taylor-Johnson

2023

Shortform

01:22

Swipe

A stylised editorial with Aaron Taylor-Johnson for Icon Magazine, set in a dystopian world that blurs the line between fashion and narrative. Through stark environments, textured lighting, and a cinematic edge, the visuals explore control, rebellion and identity - placing Aaron at the centre of a world that feels both fractured and hyper-designed. A bold, atmospheric take on modern masculinity.
